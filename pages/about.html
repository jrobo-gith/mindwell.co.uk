About

I am qualified person-centred counsellor based in Warwickshire but able to work online anywhere in the UK. Person-Centred counselling provides a supportive and non-judgemental space for you to talk openly about anything and everything that might be troubling you at that moment in time. My role is to support you in finding answers to the things that are troubling you and to guide you in building resilience so you can deal with whatever comes your way in the future.

My personal experiences and training have given me a broad understanding of life and emotions which has led me to a non-judgmental down-to-earth approach. My areas of professional expertise include depression, anxiety, stress, relationships, self-esteem, parent and child relationships, self harming, loss and trauma. I believe in personal growth and I want to empower my clients to gain knowledge, self awareness and self-confidence.
